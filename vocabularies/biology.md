# nocturnal
**詞性**: adjective | ⭐⭐⭐⭐
**定義**: 夜行性的 / active at night
**例句**: 
- "Owls are nocturnal birds that hunt in complete darkness." (貓頭鷹是在完全黑暗中捕獵的夜行性鳥類。)
- "The nocturnal animals in the zoo are most active after sunset." (動物園裡的夜行性動物在日落後最為活躍。)
**常見搭配**:
- nocturnal animals ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- nocturnal habits ⭐⭐⭐⭐
- nocturnal species ⭐⭐⭐⭐
**語法**: 通常放在名詞前作定語
**記憶**: noct- = night (拉丁文夜晚) → 反義詞: diurnal (日行性的)

**定義**: 負鼠 / marsupial mammals, particularly Australian brushtail possums
**例句**: 
- "Possums were introduced to New Zealand for the fur trade." (負鼠為毛皮貿易而被引進紐西蘭。)
- "Possums compete with native birds for food." (負鼠與本地鳥類競爭食物。)
**常見搭配**:
- possum control ⭐⭐⭐⭐
- brushtail possum ⭐⭐⭐⭐
- introduced possums ⭐⭐⭐
**語法**: 可數名詞，單數 possum
**記憶**: 在紐澳指 brushtail possum；在美國 opossum 是不同物種

# intensively
**詞性**: adverb | ⭐⭐⭐⭐
**定義**: 密集地；加強地 / in a concentrated or thorough way
**例句**: 
- "The birds were intensively managed to ensure survival." (這些鳥類被密集管理以確保存活。)
- "Students studied intensively for the final exams." (學生們為期末考試密集學習。)
**常見搭配**:
- intensively managed ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- study/work intensively ⭐⭐⭐⭐
- intensively farmed ⭐⭐⭐⭐
**語法**: 修飾動詞
**記憶**: intensive (密集的) + -ly；同義詞: thoroughly, closely, rigorously

- supplementary materials ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- supplementary feeding ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- supplementary income ⭐⭐⭐⭐
- supplementary information ⭐⭐⭐⭐
**語法**: 常作定語修飾名詞
**記憶**: supplement (補充) + -ary；同義詞: additional, extra, complementary

# hand-raising
**詞性**: noun/adjective | ⭐⭐⭐
**定義**: 人工餵養；手養 / raising animals by hand rather than by their parents
**例句**: 
- "Hand-raising chicks increased their survival rate." (人工餵養雛鳥提高了它們的存活率。)
- "The zoo specializes in hand-raising orphaned animals." (這個動物園專門人工餵養孤兒動物。)
**常見搭配**:
- hand-raising chicks ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- hand-raising program ⭐⭐⭐⭐
- hand-raised animals ⭐⭐⭐⭐
**語法**: 可作名詞或形容詞；連字符連接
**記憶**: hand (手) + raising (養育) = 用手養育；同義詞: hand-rearing

# accommodate
**詞性**: verb | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
**定義**: 容納；為...提供空間 / to provide space or room for
**例句**: 
- "The hotel can accommodate up to 500 guests." (這家酒店可以容納多達500位客人。)
- "The habitat must accommodate the growing population." (棲息地必須容納不斷增長的種群。)
**常見搭配**:
- accommodate guests ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- accommodate needs ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- accommodate growth ⭐⭐⭐⭐
- accommodate up to ⭐⭐⭐⭐
**語法**: 雙寫 m：accommodate（常見拼寫錯誤）
**記憶**: ac- (朝向) + com- (一起) + mod (measure) = 調整以容納；也指「遷就；適應」

- stakeholder meeting ⭐⭐⭐⭐

**記憶**: stake (利害關係) + holder (持有者) = 有利害關係的人；商業/政策常用詞

# preservation
**詞性**: noun | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
**定義**: 保護；保存 / the act of keeping something safe from harm
**例句**: 
- "Wildlife preservation is crucial for maintaining biodiversity." (野生動物保護對維持生物多樣性至關重要。)
- "The preservation of endangered species requires global cooperation." (瀕危物種的保護需要全球合作。)
**常見搭配**:
- preservation of species ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- wildlife preservation ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- cultural preservation ⭐⭐⭐⭐
- food preservation ⭐⭐⭐⭐
**語法**: 不可數名詞
**記憶**: preserve (保存) + -ation；同義詞: conservation, protection；區別: preservation 強調維持原狀，conservation 強調明智使用
