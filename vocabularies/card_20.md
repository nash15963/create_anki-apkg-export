# 1. accomplishment

### 1. **成就、成果**
- **定義**：成功完成某事後所獲得的成就或成果
- **英英定義**：Something that has been achieved successfully, especially through skill or effort
- **例句**："Getting her PhD was her greatest accomplishment."
- **例句**："The team celebrated their accomplishment of finishing the project ahead of schedule."
- **使用情境**：用於描述個人或團體透過努力達成的重要目標或里程碑

### 2. **才能、技藝**
- **定義**：某人所具備的特殊技能或才華
- **英英定義**：A skill or special ability acquired through training or practice
- **例句**："Playing the piano is one of her many accomplishments."
- **例句**："His accomplishments include fluency in five languages."
- **使用情境**：用於履歷或介紹某人時，描述其所擁有的專業技能

### 常見搭配：
- great accomplishment（偉大的成就）
- sense of accomplishment（成就感）
- major accomplishment（重大成就）
- personal accomplishment（個人成就）

### 語法提醒：
- 名詞（可數）：accomplishment, accomplishments
- 動詞形式：accomplish（完成、達成）
- 形容詞：accomplished（有成就的、熟練的）
