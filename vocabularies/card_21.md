# squat

### 1. **蹲下;蹲坐**
- **定義**：彎曲膝蓋使身體降低,臀部接近地面但不坐下
- **英英定義**：to crouch or sit with one's knees bent and one's heels close to or touching one's buttocks or the back of one's thighs
- **例句**："The children squatted down to look at the insects on the ground."
- **例句**："He squatted beside the campfire to warm his hands."
- **使用情境**：描述身體動作,特別是在沒有椅子或需要降低身體高度時

### 2. **非法佔用(空置建築)**
- **定義**：未經許可佔用空置的建築物或土地
- **英英定義**：to unlawfully occupy an uninhabited building or settle on a piece of land
- **例句**："Homeless people have been squatting in the abandoned warehouse."
- **例句**："The family squatted in the empty house for three months."
- **使用情境**：討論住房問題、社會議題或財產權時使用

### 3. **矮胖的(形容詞)**
- **定義**：形容某物又矮又寬
- **英英定義**：short and thick; low and wide
- **例句**："The squat building looked out of place among the tall skyscrapers."
- **例句**："She picked up a squat ceramic vase from the shelf."
- **使用情境**：描述建築物、物體或人的外形特徵

### 常見搭配：
- squat down (蹲下)
- do squats (做深蹲運動)
- squat position (蹲姿)
- squat thrust (深蹲推舉)
- squat rack (深蹲架)

### 語法提醒：
- 原形：squat
- 第三人稱單數：squats
- 過去式：squatted
- 現在分詞：squatting
- 形容詞形式：squat (無變化)
