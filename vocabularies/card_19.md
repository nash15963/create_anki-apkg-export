- **定義**：詢問什麼事情讓某人擔憂或困擾
- **英英定義**：asking what is troubling or concerning someone
- **例句**："You look stressed. What's weighing on you?"
- **例句**："What's weighing on your mind lately?"
- **使用情境**：關心他人、詢問煩惱

### 常見搭配：
- weighing on your mind（壓在心頭）
- weighing heavily（沉重壓著）
- something's weighing on me（有事困擾我）

### 語法提醒：
- 動詞片語：weigh on
- 過去式：weighed on
- 現在分詞：weighing on
- 類似表達：What's bothering you?
