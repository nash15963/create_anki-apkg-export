# downgraded

### 1. **降級、降低評級**
- **定義**：降低等級、品質或重要性
- **英英定義**：to reduce to a lower grade, rank, or level of importance
- **例句**："The hurricane was downgraded from category 5 to category 3"
- **例句**："The company's credit rating was downgraded by Moody's"
- **使用情境**：金融評級、天氣預報、職位調整等正式場合

### 2. **降低品質或功能**
- **定義**：使品質或功能變差
- **英英定義**：to replace with something inferior or less advanced
- **例句**："I downgraded my phone plan to save money"
- **例句**："The software was downgraded to the previous version due to bugs"

### 常見搭配：
- downgraded rating（降低的評級）
- downgraded forecast（下調的預測）
- downgraded status（降低的地位）
- downgraded version（降級版本）

### 語法提醒：
- 原形：downgrade
- 第三人稱單數：downgrades
- 過去式：downgraded
- 現在分詞：downgrading
